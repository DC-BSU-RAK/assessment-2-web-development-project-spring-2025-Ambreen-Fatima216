About the Game

Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a legendary mobile strategy game developed by Supercell. Launched on August 2, 2012 for iOS and on October 7, 2013 for Android, the game transformed mobile gaming with its addictive blend of base-building, real-time battles, and clan warfare.

Players begin with a small village, nurturing it into a fortress of might. Through gold, elixir, and dark elixir, armies are trained and defenses strengthened. With each raid and defense, strategy sharpens. But the true power lies in clans â€” where friendships form, wars are fought, and glory is shared.

From humble beginnings to a global esports spectacle, Clash of Clans has remained a dominant force for over a decade. Supercell continues to deliver game-changing updates and seasonal events, making the battlefield feel fresh and fierce.

Milestones of Mayhem

  • 2012: Global launch on iOS. The world begins to Clash.
  • 2013: Android release opens floodgates to millions more warriors.
  • 2015: Clan Wars become the ultimate test of coordination and strategy.
  • 2017: Builder Base shakes the formula with new mechanics and units.
  • 2019: Clan War Leagues and the rise of competitive play.
  • 2022: Town Hall 15 arrives with electrifying power.
  • 2024: Clash Anytime Update brings unmatched flexibility and progress pacing.
